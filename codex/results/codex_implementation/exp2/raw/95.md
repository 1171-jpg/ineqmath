\[

For \( a \) to be positive, the discriminant of this quadratic must be non-negative. The discriminant \( D \) is given by:

\[
D = (-4b)^2 - 4(1)(b^2 + 3) = 16b^2 - 4(b^2 + 3) = 16b^2 - 4b^2 - 12 = 12b^2 - 12 = 12(b^2 - 1).

\[
b^2 - 1 \geq 0 \implies b \geq 1.

2\sqrt{2ab} - \frac{1}{2}a - b \quad ? \quad \frac{1}{2}.
