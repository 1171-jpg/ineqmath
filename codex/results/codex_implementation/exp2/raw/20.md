$$

**Right-Hand Side (RHS):**
\[
RHS = C \sqrt{\frac{ab + bc + ca}{(a+b)(b+c)(c+a)}}

We need to find the largest constant \(C\) such that:
\[
LHS \geq C \cdot RHS

Using the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality on the LHS, we have:
\[

\[
