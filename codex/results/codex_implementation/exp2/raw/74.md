### Step 2: Apply the Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality
We can apply the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality in the following form:

\[
\left( \sum_{i=1}^n x_i \cdot y_i \right)^2 \leq \left( \sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2 \right) \left( \sum_{i=1}^n y_i^2 \right)

\[
