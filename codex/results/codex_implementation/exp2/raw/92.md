\[
\frac{a}{2a^2 + 1} + \frac{4b}{3b^2 + 4} \quad () \quad \frac{7}{5}

\[

\]

Thus,

\[
\frac{a}{2a^2 + 1} \leq \frac{a}{2\sqrt{2}a} = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}.
\]

\[
\frac{4b}{3b^2 + 4} \leq \frac{4b}{4\sqrt{3}b} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}.

\[
