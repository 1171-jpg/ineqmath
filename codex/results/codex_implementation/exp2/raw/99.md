## Problem

$$
\frac{1}{1+a^2} + \frac{1}{3(1+b)} + \frac{1}{1+c^2} \quad () \quad \frac{7}{4}.
$$

Determine the correct inequality relation to fill in the blank.

Options:

(A) $\leq$ 

(B) $\geq$

(C) $=$ 

(D) $<$

(E) $>$

(F) None of the above
